Motor Efficiency Mapping

Recovering a purchased traction motor's real efficiency at operating points the datasheet never covered.

Problem
The traction motor we sourced only listed nominal figures — no efficiency data at our actual load and speed points.
Approach
Building a 2D finite-element model in ANSYS Maxwell — refining the air-gap mesh and sweeping operating points to produce a full efficiency map and cogging-torque profile.
Result
Efficiency map and cogging-torque results — in progress, to be added.
Learned
How air-gap mesh quality drives the accuracy of torque and loss predictions.
